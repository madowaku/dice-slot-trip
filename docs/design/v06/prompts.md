# v0.6 Visual Target Prompts

Mode: built-in `image_gen`, one independent generation call per option. Use case: `ui-mockup`. Native 941×1672 outputs were resized with Lanczos to 720×1280; no generated content was composited or repainted.

Shared state in all prompts: Cairo, lap 4, HP 2/3, PB -2.4s, main 18/32, cat in an 8-stop loop with EXIT 4, slot `[6][6][_]`, third roll next, and exactly one READY die in the fixed bottom tray. Every prompt requires readable main route, branch/bypass, loop, and boss gate; forbids a generic dashboard, excessive cards, 90 spaces, multiple dice, modal, and watermark. Generated lettering is illustrative only and must be replaced by Godot Control/Label text in implementation.

## A — Sunlit Cairo Diorama

Premium one-handed portrait gameplay mockup. Sunlit Cairo miniature 2.5D diorama with sandstone streets, market awnings, palms and oasis water. Compact fixed top HUD, large unobstructed central map, fixed bottom tray. Handcrafted sandstone route tiles; parchment, aged brass and turquoise enamel. Travel atmosphere is primary, but phone-size route topology and stopping choices remain clear.

## B — Explorer's Atlas

Premium one-handed portrait gameplay mockup using mapmaking clarity rather than a 3D diorama. Explorer's folding Cairo atlas on a travel desk: ivory paper, hand-inked cartography, compass rose, route stamps, faint Nile and district sketches. Slim top ribbon HUD, dominant central atlas, fixed field-kit tray. Main route, branch, bypass and loop use distinct line styles; boss gate is a wax seal. Sandstone, indigo ink, turquoise highlight and coral hazard accents.

## C — Lantern Night Bazaar

Premium one-handed portrait gameplay mockup emphasizing anticipation of the third roll. Cairo market at blue hour, elevated route through bazaar rooftops, indigo sky, warm lantern pools and Nile reflections. Minimal top instrument strip, large central stage-like map, substantial fixed leather-and-wood tray. Indigo fabric, hammered copper, lantern glass, carved wood and turquoise navigation light. The empty third slot glows and connects visually to the single READY die.
